Our Story

Born from a love
of the perfect cup

HSVV COFFEE began in a small corner of the city when founder Maria Santos returned from a trip through the highlands of Ethiopia and Colombia, carrying nothing but a suitcase and an obsession with extraordinary coffee.

Today, we partner directly with over 20 farms across three continents — paying fair premiums, visiting annually, and ensuring every harvest meets our exacting standards before a single bean touches our roaster.
